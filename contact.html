Frequently Asked Questions

Is this news actually real? +

Yes! Every story we feature is sourced from reputable news outlets. We link to the original sources so you can verify for yourself. We're optimistic, not delusional.

How do you choose what to cover? +

Our AI scours 50+ news sources and filters for stories that are genuinely positive, substantive, and verifiable. We skip the clickbait, the disguised bad news, and the trivial fluff. Only the good stuff makes the cut.

Are you ignoring bad news? +

Nope. We're not pretending problems don't exist. We're just focusing on what's working, because that's what's missing from most news coverage. Knowing what's possible helps us fix what's broken.
